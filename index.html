About Me

Julius Githaiga (Mr.).

Hello I'm Julius, a web and mobile applications developer (software developer) from Nairobi, Kenya.

I studied Computer Science in Kenyatta University.

The software development tools that I oftenly use include (but not limited to) Java, Groovy, Grails, Android, NativeScript, Cordova, MYSQL, PostgreSQL, NoSQL (MongoDB and CouchDB), AngularJS/Angular2, NodeJS, JavaScript, JQuery, JSON, and XML. I have also worked with several existing software APIs such as Twilio and Cheddargetter.

What I Do

Web Applications Development

Mobile first design-first mantra is always emphasized (unless my client explicitly requests otherwise, of which I don't recommend). I build fast-loading, highly optimized experiences that will work across a wide range of devices. This responsive design and development will help make your website easily accessible across all devices.

Mobile Applications Development

I work with my clients to build robust, award-winning cross platform mobile solutions. The mobile apps I develop work seemslessly on iOS, Android, Windows 8, HTML5, Firefox OS, and other platforms. I also develop native android apps.

Content Management Systems Implementation

I believe my clients should always have access (and ability) to easily change content on their website. The right content management system can give you (and your staff) control over content that needs to change more regularly. This will enable you manage your Blog, News, Photos, or Videos easily without the need to learn programming.
